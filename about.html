ABOUT


Copper Bay Candles is small business established in 2018. We offer a range of beautiful, hand poured, luxury candles, wax melts and diffusers in a wide range of sumptuous scents - all of which can be personalised! We also provide stunning wax melt burners and a range of matching hand wash. These are all the perfect addition to your home.


We only use high quality soy wax and fragrance oils in our candles which have all been tested. We believe it is important to be eco & environmentally friendly. We only use biodegradable and vegan friendly wax. No animal products are used and no animal testing is carried out in its manufacture.


We offer personalisation on all our beautiful candles which makes for the perfect gift! When sending out your order, it will be hand packaged and gift wrapped.


Each candle is made to order to ensure the highest of quality so please allow 5-10 days from purchase to receiving your item. As each candle is personally made for you, returns and refunds are not accepted unless items arrive damaged.
